``PyPlanAnalysis.plan``
========================

Main user-facing API: :class:`~PyPlanAnalysis.plan.PatientPlan` loads DICOM
data and runs the analysis; :class:`~PyPlanAnalysis.plan.AnalysisResults`
holds the metrics, plots, and NTCP outputs and exports them.
